Hello! I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Information Studies, University College London (UCL), UK.
Here is a space where I hope to share my thoughts, ideas and progress on research.

My research aims to facilitate multiple interpretations of museum objects through methods related to semantic technoloiges, incluidng semantics-based information modelling and enrichment. The research is based on concepts of the value of museum objects as documents (or information carriers), and the polysemy of objects, i.e. an object can hold multiple, sometimes even conflicting meanings contingent on the contexts of interpretation. The research aims to represent the information about museum objects in a way that allows interpretation through pluralistic perspectives, especially perspectives in material culture studies, and to reveal the absent or implicit information about objects where available.

My research interests lie in:
- Cultural heritage data infrastructure
- Ontology
- Linked data
- Semantic technologies for digital humanities
- Museum studies
- Digital humanities
- Knowledge organisation and representation
