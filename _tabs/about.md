**Hello!** I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Information Studies, University College London (UCL), UK. I hold a MSc in Digtial humanities and a BA in English (with personal specificaiton in linguistics).

Here is a space where I share my **thoughts, ideas and progress in research**.

My doctoral research investigates how to facilitate multiple interpretations of museum objects through methods related to semantic technoloiges, incluidng semantics-based information modelling and enrichment. As digital humanities research, it intersects with museum studies, material culture studies, critical data studies, semantic technologies, and knowledge organisation and representation.

My research interests lie in:
- Cultural heritage data infrastructure
- Ontology
- Linked data
- Semantic technologies for digital humanities
- Museum studies
- Digital humanities
- Knowledge organisation and representation
